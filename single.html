REALISTIC KEYBOARD ON TOUCHSCREENS

Feel the virtual keyaboard on your smartphone like your physical keyboard.

March 30, 2025 Milad Jamalzadeh

Virtual Keyboard

You didn't feel anything when you typed? That's normal. Virtual buttons often lack haptic feedback. To feel the buttons, haptic feedback needs to be integrated. Smartphones may offer simple vibrations, but these don't feel natural. During my collaboration with VibraNova, we developed touchscreens capable of generating complex, realistic haptic feedback.

By the way, did you know that different fingers have varying levels of sensitivity to haptic feedback? This means that if you want all the buttons to feel the same, you'll need to adjust the intensity of the haptic feedback based on which finger is being used. Do you know which finger is the most sensitive?

Contact me if you would like to know how to have realistic haptic feedback on your user interface and how it can improve user experience. Your customers can even feel the texture of your products from distance with haptic feedback during online shopping!
